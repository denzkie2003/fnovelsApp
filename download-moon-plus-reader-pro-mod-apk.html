What is Moon+ Reader Pro?

In the age of information technology boom, we often choose to read books with mobile phones instead of bulky paper books, making it difficult to move. The phone is like a portable bookcase, it can store thousands of books, allowing you to easily manage, store and read at any time. Do not worry about book preservation, or the fact that some pages of the book are torn during use. With just one phone, all problems can be solved. But you don’t know which application to choose the best to read book on smartphone.

Moon+ Reader Pro is the leading reading app on Android phones, released by Moon+. It is like a Kindle, allowing you to read your text files easily. Besides, the application offers a variety of features to help you customize PDF files, making the reading process more comfortable. With the aim of giving readers an experience like reading a real book, Moon+ Reader Pro allows you to easily store, highlight, bookmark, rotate pages, …
